About Me

My interest in technology began with curiosity about how things work and how magical they are, from just basic html in highschool that strengthened even more with hello world of c and some of highs school research related to AI and programming. This curiosity led me to pursue Computer Science, where I continue to challenge myself with both academic coursework and self-study in areas like calculus, physics, and programming frameworks.

Over time, I’ve built skills in C, C++, Java, Python, SQL, and web technologies, while also exploring tools such as Git/GitHub, Flask, and Django. I enjoy creating projects that combine theory with practical applications, and I’m eager to keep expanding my knowledge to tackle real-world challenges.

As part of my academic journey, I had the opportunity to work on a senior high school capstone project that was recognized for its quality. Experiences like this motivate me to keep learning, creating, improving, and finding new ways to innovate.
